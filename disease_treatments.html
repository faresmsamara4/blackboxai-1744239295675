Disease Treatment Guide

Common Cold

Home Treatment

  • Rest and stay hydrated
  • Warm salt water gargles
  • Steam inhalation
  • Over-the-counter pain relievers

Professional Treatment

  • Antiviral medications (if flu)
  • Prescription decongestants
  • Antibiotics (only if bacterial infection develops)

Natural Remedies

  • Ginger tea with honey
  • Chicken soup
  • Vitamin C supplements

Influenza (Flu)

Home Treatment

  • Bed rest and fluids
  • OTC fever reducers
  • Warm compresses for muscle aches

Professional Treatment

  • Antiviral drugs (Tamiflu, Relenza)
  • Prescription cough suppressants
  • Hospitalization for severe cases

Natural Remedies

  • Elderberry syrup
  • Echinacea tea
  • Garlic supplements

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Home Treatment

  • Reduce sodium intake
  • Regular exercise
  • Stress management techniques

Professional Treatment

  • ACE inhibitors
  • Beta blockers
  • Diuretics

Natural Remedies

  • Hibiscus tea
  • Garlic supplements
  • Omega-3 fatty acids

Diabetes Type 2

Home Treatment

  • Blood sugar monitoring
  • Low-carb diet
  • Regular physical activity

Professional Treatment

  • Metformin
  • Insulin therapy
  • GLP-1 receptor agonists

Natural Remedies

  • Cinnamon supplements
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Aloe vera juice

Acid Reflux (GERD)

Home Treatment

  • Smaller, frequent meals
  • Avoid trigger foods (spicy, acidic)
  • Elevate head while sleeping

Professional Treatment

  • Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs)
  • H2 blockers
  • Surgery for severe cases

Natural Remedies

  • Aloe vera juice
  • Ginger tea
  • Slippery elm

Eczema (Atopic Dermatitis)

Home Treatment

  • Moisturize frequently
  • Oatmeal baths
  • Cool compresses

Professional Treatment

  • Topical corticosteroids
  • Calcineurin inhibitors
  • Biologic drugs

Natural Remedies

  • Coconut oil
  • Sunflower seed oil
  • Probiotics
